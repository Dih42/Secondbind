How it works?

You donate 1 box of books = we plant 10 trees
You donate 1 box of books = we will fund a classroom project
If you live in another city and have a buyback order, you may include up to 10 lbs of donated books in your current buyback order in the same box. However, if you don’t have a buyback order, then we don’t pay for shipping for donated books at this time.

Our Mission by 2022

Our main goal is to save and plant as many trees as we can and also provide each customer with the opportunity to turn their unwanted textbooks into profit. Donating books allows a chance for a second life, giving someone else the opportunity to read and enjoy the book. It’s also an environmentally-conscious decision since recycling prevents them from ending up in a landfill.
